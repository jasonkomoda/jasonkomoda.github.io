Color Pass

This is a somewhat complicated game and requires players to think a lot while they play. In this game the player moves the black circle through a certain color on the bar as it moves down the screen. "Color" or "word" decide what color the the player should move the circle through. "Color" means the color of the word presented on the screen, and "word" means the color that the world spells out. Different power ups throughout the game lets the player gain lives, add points to their score, and slow the movement speed of the bar down.

Spidey-Sense

In this game the player's main goal is to kill Batman by hitting him with spider webs. In order to survive, the player must dodge knives thrown by Batman and kill goblins before they walk off the screen.

CollectNCapture

In this third-person shooter/objective game, the player must collect spheres throughout the map and kill enemies in order to complete objectives. Eventually, the player will be able to exit the tunnel and explore the outside world through more quests.

Ski Royale

This game is both a single player and multiplayer split screen racing/combat skiing game. Players pick up items and bash each other with their poles in order to get to the finish line first. Xbox controllers are ideal, however, it's still playable on a keyboard.
