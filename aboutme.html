Hey! My name is Jason Komoda and I am currently a senior at the University of Denver majoring in Animation and Game Development (Computer Science), while minoring in Mathematics and Emergent Digital Practices. Born and raised on the small island of Oahu, Hawaii, it was always a dream of mine to make video games for a living, and till this day, I find that opportunity too cool to pass up.

Throughout my life, I've always had a passion for video games. Eventually I ended up loving FPS and MOBA games like Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 2 (best one ever), Overwatch, CS:GO, and League of Legends. Besides playing and making video games, some of my hobbies include going fishing with friends, skiing, chilling at the beach, and listening to electronic/tropical house music.

Let me know if you have any questions or comments by contacting me through the info on the home page. Thanks for checking my page out, hope you enjoy!
